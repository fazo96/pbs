How to read the Graph

Arrow: every arrow is an activity and the ID and duration (in days) are shown over the arrow, like this: activityID (minimumDuration/maximumDuration)

The color of the arrow tells the type of the activity:

  • Red Arrow: critical activity
  • Green Arrow: free delay of an activity, expressed this way: activityID (freeDelay)
  • Yellow Arrow: activity with a free delay of 0
  • Blue Arrow: activity with a free delay over 0

Every circle represents the day of the start of one or more activities and/or the day of the end of one or more activities.
