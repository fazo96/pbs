Welcome to PBS Wizard

This web application will supposedly help you manage your Project Breakdown Structure.

What can it do

  • You can use the RawEditor to import and export your PBS data. This software is still a prototype, so be careful!
  • You can use the Editor to more easily edit your Project Activities and Resources.
  • With the Activities and Resources pages you can view detailed info about your Activities and Resources.
  • Pert and Gantt are self-explanatory, try them!
